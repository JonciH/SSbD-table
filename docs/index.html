Safe & Sustainable by Design — EU Horizon portfolio

SSbD Project Tracker

Following the last SSbD meeting in Brussels by the European Commission, there was a clear call for stronger coordination across RTD activities. In response, I've created a sortable, filterable reference table of EU Horizon-funded projects working within the Safe and Sustainable by Design (SSbD) framework, inspired by Fantke, P. Sustain. Chem. Pharm. 2025, 45, 101986.

Designed for SSbD practitioners, Horizon consortium partners, industrial stakeholders, and European Commission project officers, the table provides a single, searchable overview of who is working on what in the SSbD landscape.

— Jonas Hoffmann, GreenDelta, Sustainability Researcher

About this table. Acronyms, titles, websites, and coordinators were compiled manually from public SSbD project communications. Funding call/topic ID, start/end dates, coordinator country, and budget figures are pulled from CORDIS, the European Commission's research results database. Partner-role columns (technology development, risk assessment, environmental/social LCA, AI, MCDA scoring, industrial use cases, dissemination, exploitation) are not tagged in CORDIS by role and are filled in through manual review — these are the fields most likely to need a community correction.

Spot something missing or wrong? Open an issue using the template below and it will be reviewed before merging into the dataset.
